Privacy Policy

This Chrome Extension respects your privacy. We are committed to protecting any information that may be collected during the use of this extension.

Information Collected

During usage, the extension may send requests to our server which log technical details including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device info (User-Agent)
  • Request URL and method
  • Submitted data, if applicable (e.g. form input)

This information is logged temporarily for debugging and performance monitoring purposes only. It is not used to personally identify users and is not stored long-term.

Data Storage and Use

The logged information is not stored in any database or shared with third parties. The extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information unless explicitly stated and with user consent.

Third-Party Sharing

No data is sold or shared with advertisers or other third parties.

Permissions

The extension may request certain Chrome permissions (e.g. access to specific websites or tabs) solely for functionality purposes. These permissions are not used to track or collect user data.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the extension indicates your acceptance of the current policy.
